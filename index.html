About Our Mission

Who We Are

Comfort Light House is a non-profit organization based in Ogun State, Nigeria, dedicated to improving the lives of the less privileged through various humanitarian initiatives.

Since our founding in 2010, we've impacted over 10,000 lives through our food distribution programs, educational support, and healthcare initiatives.
